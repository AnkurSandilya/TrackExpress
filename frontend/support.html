Delivery Help & Support

Why is my delivery delayed?

Delays can occur due to weather, route congestion, or high parcel volume at transit hubs.

What does "In Transit" mean?

Your parcel is currently moving between cities or logistics hubs towards the destination.

What does "Out for Delivery" indicate?

The parcel is with the delivery agent and will be delivered soon.

Can I change delivery address?

Address changes are only allowed before dispatch. After that, changes may not be possible.

Tracking status not updating?

Status updates pause when parcels travel between hubs. Please check again later.

Estimated delivery time?

It depends on distance, package weight, and transit routing.
